Marvel vs DC

My Love for Comics

I always loved watching cartoons as a kid.  Watching Marvel Xmen, DC Superman and Batman were some of my go to shows.  As a teen, I went to my first San Diego Comic Con (before it was a "thing") and was super stoked to meet Lou Ferrigno (aka the original 1970s Incredible Hulk) back in 2004.  After my son was born, I had the priviledge to continue and share my love for it, enjoying them together as I read the comics to him, watched newer shows such as Super Hero Squad, Teen Titans Go and movies with new universes such as Next Avengers: Heroes of Tomorrow.  Fast forward to present time, there are a plethora of multiverse and characters with their own movies and shows we enjoy watching as a family.  Without further ado, here are my top three picks of characters from both Marvel and DC.

Marvel

?

Captain America

He has honor, is loyal, humble, strong, and patriotic.

?

Hulk

He's a gentle intellect when calm, a super strong being when emotionally charged. He has healed a whole planet with his DNA. And he's green, my favorite color.

?

Captain Marvel

She's super quick, strong, loyal, can fly and breathe in outer space and can instantly destroy opposing spaceships.

DC

?

Wonder Woman

Noble fighting for truth and peace, highly trained, disciplined, super strong, nearly invulnerable to physical harm.

?

Harley Quinn

She was loyal to her man, has brain and beauty

?

Poison Ivy

She loves the environment..and my favorite color is green.

In Closing....

Out of the two comics, my favorite comic would have to be Marvel, as DC tend to get too dark in story for me.   There were too many choices for my third pick in the Marvel category.   It was a close call between Thor, Iron Man, Spiderman, The Phoenix, and Shuri.   I ultimately chose Captain Marvel.  For DC I almost chose Superman, but Captain Marvel is sort of similar, only female.  And who ever said villain characters couldn't have a place in my heart anyway.  Had to make sure I mention them.   Because without a villain, who would need a super hero?
